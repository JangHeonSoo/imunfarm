## 1. Total Subsidy Scale — 40-60 Million KRW Possible for 100kW

Put National + Local + Financial Support together and shrink installation costs by 25-35%.

- Convergence Support (National): Up to 35 million KRW
- Rural Solar Grant: Around 20 million KRW
- Local Municipal Grant: 5-15 million KRW (varies by region)
- Financial Aid (Low-interest loan): 2% range, 5-year grace, 10-year repayment
- REC Multiplier Benefit: Extra 10 million KRW/year revenue

Actual Receipt Examples:

- Nonsan City (100kW): Convergence 30M + Local 10M = 40 million KRW. Actual burden 130M KRW.
- Gochang County: Rural Solar 20M + Convergence 25M = 45 million KRW.

## 2. Convergence Support Project — #1 Grant for Agrivoltaics (30-35 Million KRW)

Hosted by Korea Energy Agency. Covers up to 50% of total project cost (70% for exterior walls). 2026 budget massively expanded.

Eligibility:

- Farmers adding solar + crops on their own land (under 500kW).
- Active cultivators (registered resident >1 year, >1.2M KRW annual revenue).
- Village cooperatives take priority.

2026 Unit Price (100kW basis, excl. VAT):

- Solar facility: 350,000 KRW/kW support → 35 million KRW.
- Total including monitoring: 30-35 million KRW.

Application Window: February - May announcements. First-come, first-served. Select contractor, then apply via BPM (electronic civil service).

Actual Example: 2025 Yeongam farm, received 32M KRW in convergence funding for 100kW. Installation dropped from 170M → 138M.

## 3. Rural Solar Support Project — Farmer Exclusive (Around 20 Million KRW)

Ministry of Agriculture + Energy Agency. Farmer verification support.

Target:

- Livestock barns, agrivoltaic farmland.
- Installed within 5km of residence.
- Cooperatives up to 1.5MW.

Support Amount: 200,000 - 300,000 KRW per kW. For 100kW, that's 20-30 million KRW.

- 2026 Expansion: Up to 1,000kW for RE100 companies/factories.

Application: Energy Agency website, early year announcements.

Can overlap with national funds. Rural-focused.

## 5. Financial Aid — Business Without Cash (Low-interest loan)

Renewable Energy Financial Support: 2.0-2.5% per year, 5-year grace, 15-year repayment.

- Loan limit: Around 1 billion KRW.
- Guarantee: KIBO 90% guarantee.
- 100kW Example: Fully finance 170M KRW. Monthly repayment 1.5M KRW (after grace).

2026 Change: RE100/Industrial complex expanded to 1,000kW, factories added.

## 6. REC Multiplier Benefits — Extra 10M KRW Annual Profit

Agrivoltaic multiplier holds at 1.5x. 100kW generates 130,000 kWh annually.

- Normal REC: 72,000 KRW/MWh × 130 = 9.36 million KRW.
- Agrivoltaic: 1.5x = 14.04 million KRW.

Total Annual Revenue: SMP 15M + REC 14M + Farming 3M = 32 million KRW.

## 7. Qualifications & Application Tips — Don't Miss Out

Qualifications:

- Active farmer (agricultural management registration).
- Residency >1 year, revenue proof.
- Select a participating contractor (Energy Agency website list).

Application Steps:

1. Check Energy Agency website announcements (Feb-Mar).
2. Choose contractor → Get quota.
3. BPM electronic application (upload documents).
4. Pass screening → Install → Final inspection → Receive funds.

Period: Within 1 month of announcement. First-come, first-served.

Note: Convergence + Rural Solar cannot overlap. Aim for convergence first to max out.
